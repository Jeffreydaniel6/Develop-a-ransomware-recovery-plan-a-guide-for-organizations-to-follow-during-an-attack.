Ransomware Recovery Plan

Follow these steps to prepare, detect, respond, and recover from ransomware attacks.

  • Regular employee training and awareness campaigns.
  • Patch management and software updates.
  • Secure remote access with VPNs and endpoint protection.
  • Incident simulation and tabletop exercises.

  • Regular log analysis with automated tools.
  • Threat intelligence integration for proactive alerts.
  • Automated threat hunting for suspicious activity.
  • Maintain a comprehensive inventory of digital assets.

  • Quarantine affected devices immediately.
  • Conduct internal communication to avoid panic.
  • Verify data backups to ensure integrity.
  • Deploy endpoint detection and response (EDR) solutions.

  • Verify the system’s integrity post-cleanup.
  • Gradual restoration from backups.
  • Conduct a full post-incident review and reporting.
  • Implement lessons learned to prevent recurrence.

  • Notify stakeholders, including customers if required.
  • Engage law enforcement and share IOCs.
  • Avoid public disclosure unless necessary.
